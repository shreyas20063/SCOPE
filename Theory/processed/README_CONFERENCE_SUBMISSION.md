## Key Innovation Claims (Use in Conference Abstract)

1. **Audio Synthesis of Mathematics**: Real-time synthesized impulse responses make abstract transfer functions audible. No existing educational platform does this.

2. **Cross-Domain Equivalence**: Same differential equation shows identical behavior across Mechanical, Electrical, Acoustic, Thermal domains with synchronized audio playback. Students hear that mathematics is universal.

3. **Deconvolution as Investigation**: Convolution Detective gamifies the inverse problem (system identification) through a detective game interface. Teaches realistic engineering problem-solving.

4. **Multisensory Harmonic Composition**: Drag harmonic sliders, hear waveform synthesize in real-time, watch 3D harmonic space and spectrum update simultaneously. First tool combining auditory + visual + kinesthetic feedback.

5. **Real-World Physics Connection**: Fourier Transform Crystallographer connects pure math to X-ray crystallography, MRI imaging, optical diffraction. Students see why Fourier matters in real physics.
